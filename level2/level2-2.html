Spellbound

By Emily Bronte


  1.         The night is darkening round me,

  2. The

    wild winds

    coldly blow;



  3.         But a tyrant spell has bound me

  4. And I cannot, cannot go.



  5.         The giant trees are bending

  6. Their

    bare boughs

    weighed with snow.



  7.         And the storm is fast descending,

  8. And yet I cannot go.


  9.         

    Clouds

    beyond

    clouds

    above me,

  10. Wastes

    beyond

    wastes

    below;



  11.         But nothing drear can move me;

  12. I will not, cannot go.
