Spellbound

By Emily Bronte


  1.         The night is darkening round me,

  2. The

    wild winds

    coldly blow;



  3.         But a tyrant spell has bound me

  4. And I cannot, cannot go.



    5.         The giant trees are bending

    Their

    bare boughs

    weighed with snow.



            And the storm is fast descending,

    And yet I cannot go.



            

    Clouds

    beyond

    clouds

    above me,

    Wastes

    beyond

    wastes

    below;



  5.         But nothing drear can move me;

  6. I will not, cannot go.
