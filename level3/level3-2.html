Spellbound

By Emily Bronte


  1.         The night is darkening round me,

  2. The

    wild winds

    coldly blow;



  3.         But a tyrant spell has bound me

    4. And I cannot, cannot go.



  4.         The giant trees are bending

  5. Their

    bare boughs

    weighed with snow.



  6.         And the storm is fast descending,

  7. And yet I cannot go.



  8.         

    Clouds

    beyond

    clouds

    above me,

  9. Wastes

    beyond

    wastes

    below;



  10.         But nothing drear can move me;

  11. I will not, cannot go.
